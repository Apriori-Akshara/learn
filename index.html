Why Reading Is Important ?

LITERARY CIRCLE

Bring your own book and flip pages with new people.

IBC brings a revolutionary change in book club meetings with a virtual set up to connect and greet people around the country and books. So, if you are looking for a book club to join but are too lazy or shy to go out, join a virtual connecting space. The book club is open for all kinds of book lovers, young, old, avid readers, or first-timers.

"Great stories are made with new people."
